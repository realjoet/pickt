What is Mindfule?

Mindfule [mind-full-e]: Short for Mindful Eating Revolution, Mindfule is a visual food diary created to start a global conversation about what's in our food and how we can improve eating habits around the world.

Based on MyPlate, Mindfule was created to educate people about what is in the food they eat. Mindfule was not created to make you lose weight. However, if you allow Mindfule to help you make better choices, Mindfule should influence weight loss through improved eating habits.

Another unique part of Mindfule is the social aspect. You can use Mindfule to track your eating habits privately, or you can share your eating habits with the world and join the revolution. By sharing different ways of approaching eating well with your teammates everyone succeeds faster. The world is on your team, and together we can revolutionize the world's eating habits!
