Be Mindfule

My name is Joe Toscano. I'm a 24 year old student at the University of Nebraska with degrees in Psychology and Advertising & Public Relations, and minors in Sociology, English and Women's & Gender Studies. In my spare time I am a personal trainer fully certified through the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM) as a Certified Personal Trainer (CPT), Performance Enhancement Specialist (PES) and Corrective Exercise Specialist (CES). Basically, I'm certified enough to train professional athletes - which I almost was and may one day be.

Oh ya, in the other part of my spare time I also played lacrosse for the University and currently coach youth in the Omaha/Lincoln Metro areas wherever I'm needed. On top of all that, I am also a former fat kid. Don't believe me?
